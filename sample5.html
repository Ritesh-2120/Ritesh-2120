Welcome to Poverty Relief Network

We aim to address poverty and support marginalized communities through a range of services including donations, clothing and food collection, educational support, medical assistance, awareness campaigns, and employment opportunities.

Donation Management

Monetary Donations: Secure payment gateway for financial contributions.

Goods and Services: System for users to donate items and services, including pick-up scheduling.

Corporate Partnerships: Tools for businesses to contribute and track their social impact.

Clothing Collection and Distribution

Clothing Drop-Off Points: Locate nearest drop-off centers or schedule home pick-ups.

Inventory Management: Track and manage collected clothing.

Distribution Network: Coordinate with local organizations to distribute clothing to those in need.

Food Redistribution

Food Collection: System for collecting surplus or unwanted food from individuals and businesses.

Food Safety: Ensure safe handling and distribution of food items.

Distribution Channels: Partner with food banks and community kitchens to distribute food.

Educational Support

Learning Resources: Access to online courses, tutoring, and vocational training.

Scholarships: Information on scholarships and educational grants.

Medical Assistance

Healthcare Services: Information on clinics, health camps, and medical aid.

Medication Aid: Connect users with programs for free or subsidized medications.

Awareness Campaigns

Educational Materials: Resources and articles on poverty issues and support services.

Community Outreach: Organize and promote local awareness events.

Employment Opportunities

Part-Time Services for Men: Opportunities for men to work as teachers, provide transportation and lifting services for donated goods, and assist in logistics.

Home-Based Work for Women: Opportunities for women to engage in home-based work like making pens, agarbatti (incense sticks), garlands, and other handicrafts. Jobs such as maid services, cleaning, and other household work catering to upper-class requirements.

Admin Dashboard

Resource Management: Oversee donations, clothing, and food distributions.

Reporting and Analytics: Track usage, impact, and operational metrics.
